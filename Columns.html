The human body is made up of 306 bones including the skull. There are two major types of skeleton which are axial and appendicular skeleton. The axial skeleton are made up of the bones of the upper body part which include cranium, scapula, nasal bone, tibia, fibula, humerus and carpals. while the appendicular skeleton consists of the lower body parts, they include femur, radius, ulna, tarsals, metatarsals, phalanges, hip bone, which tends to carry the weight of the whole body.
